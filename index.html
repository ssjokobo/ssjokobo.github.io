About Me

With a rich career spanning over a decade in the U.S., Sopon Suwannakit, a distinguished jazz pianist and educator, is currently a faculty member at Rangsit University, Thailand. Sopon's journey in jazz education is marked by his Master's degree from the University of North Texas and a Doctorate from the University of Northern Colorado. He has enriched his academic pursuits by teaching jazz at the University of Northern Colorado as a graduate teaching assistant and later as a full-time professor at Western Oregon University.

As a professional jazz pianist, Sopon has graced the stages of major cities across the globe. In the United States, he has performed in Dallas, Denver, Portland, Milwaukee, Madison, Chicago, Minneapolis, Reno, Austin, and New Orleans. Internationally, his talent has taken him to Shanghai, Penang, Yerevan, and now Bangkok. His performances and presentations are notable at prestigious festivals and conferences such as Jazz Education Network, International Society of Jazz Arrangers and Composers, Society of Ethnomusicology, International Council for Traditional Music, and Thailand International Jazz Conference.

Beyond the stage, Sopon's commitment to jazz education is evident through his founding of JazzJae+, a Facebook Page and YouTube channel, dedicated to providing jazz educational content in Thai. A highlight of his educational contributions is a series of 50 jazz history videos, spanning over 12 hours, which have significantly enriched the landscape of jazz education in Thailand.
