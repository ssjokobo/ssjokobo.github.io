About Me

With over a decade of experience in the vibrant jazz scene of the United States, Sopon Suwannakit stands as a distinguished jazz pianist and educator, a full-time faculty at Rangsit University in Thailand. Holding a Master's degree from the University of North Texas and a Doctorate from the University of Northern Colorado, Sopon's academic journey is as rich as his musical career.

Beginning as a graduate teaching assistant at the University of Northern Colorado, Sopon quickly advanced to become a full-time professor at Western Oregon University. Alongside his academic endeavors, Sopon's piano prowess has graced stages across the globe, from bustling cities in the United States like Dallas, Denver, Portland, Milwaukee, and Chicago, to international destinations like Shanghai, Penang, Yerevan, and Bangkok.

His performances and presentations at esteemed festivals and conferences such as Jazz Education Network, Society of Ethnomusicology, International Council for Traditional Music, Rocky Mountain Music Scholar's Conference, Issues in Contemporary Jazz Conference, and Thailand International Jazz Conference.

Beyond the stage, Sopon's commitment to jazz education is evident through his founding of JazzJae+, a Facebook Page and YouTube channel, dedicated to delivering jazz educational content in Thai. A standout contribution is his comprehensive series of 50 jazz history videos, totaling over 12 hours, which have become a cornerstone of jazz education in Thailand.
