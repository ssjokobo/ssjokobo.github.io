Black Orpheus (Manhã de Carnaval) - Luiz Bonfá

In a Sentimental Mood - Duke Ellington

Just Friends - John Klenner

Lullaby of Birdland - George Shearing

  • From MGM 11354 (1952) by George Shearing

Misty - Erroll Garner

  • From the album Contrasts (1954) by Erroll Garner

  • From the album Smokin' at the Half Note (1965) by Wes Montgomery and Wynton Kelly

My Romance - Richard Rodgers

On Green Dolphin Street - Bronisław Kaper

Song For My Father - Horace Silver

  • From the album Song For My Father (1965) by Horace Silver
  • From the album Love and Peace (1994) by Dee Dee Bridgewater
  • From the album Serenade for Horace (2017) by Louia Hayes (feat. Gregory Porter)

Days of Wine and Roses - Henry Mancini

The Girl From Ipanema - Antônio Carlos Jobim
