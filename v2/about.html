SOPON SUWANNAKIT

Jazz Pianist · Educator · Scholar · Bangkok

Based in Bangkok, Sopon Suwannakit is a jazz pianist and full-time faculty member at Rangsit University. He performs regularly at jazz clubs and swing dance events throughout the city, and is a member of Yusu Jazz Band, known for its focus on 1920s–30s jazz repertoire.

As founder of JazzJae+ (แจ๊สแจ๋), a platform dedicated to jazz education in Thai, Sopon has produced more than a hundred jazz history and theory videos that have become essential resources for learners across Thailand. He also leads the Jokobo Double Quartet — the first ensemble in Thailand to combine a jazz quartet with a string quartet.

Sopon earned his Master's degree at the University of North Texas, studying with Stefan Karlsson, a frequent pianist for Eddie Gomez. He then completed his Doctorate at the University of Northern Colorado, where his dissertation — Examining Middle Eastern Influences on Jazz in the 1950s and 1960s — traced Arabic musical concepts within jazz recordings. In 2018, a scholarship from the Armenian General Benevolent Union (AGBU) brought him to Armenia to study the duduk with Emmanuel Hovhannisyan.
